What is Kratom?

Kratom is a botanical substance derived from the leaves of a tropical tree known as Mitragyna speciosa, native to Southeast Asia.

Traditionally, the leaves have been used in various cultural practices for centuries. Today, kratom is available in different forms such as powder, capsules, and extracts.

How Does Kratom Work?

Kratom contains naturally occurring compounds that interact with certain receptors in the body. These interactions may influence how individuals experience mood, focus, or energy.

The effects can vary depending on factors such as serving size, individual sensitivity, and the specific type of kratom used.

Important Considerations

Kratom is intended for adults only and may not be appropriate for everyone. Laws and regulations regarding kratom differ by location.

It is important for individuals to research and understand their local regulations and make informed decisions.
